<p><p>Hurricane resistant glass is designed to withstand strong winds and impact from debris during a storm. There are two main types of hurricane resistant glass on the market: laminated glass and insulated laminated glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers of glass bonded together with a protective interlayer, providing durability and strength. Insulated laminated glass incorporates an additional layer of insulation between the glass panes, offering enhanced thermal performance in addition to hurricane protection. Both types of glass are crucial for ensuring safety and security in areas prone to hurricanes.</p></p>
