<p><p>Gel Batteries are a type of valve-regulated lead-acid (VRLA) battery that uses a gelified electrolyte to immobilize the sulfuric acid solution. This design makes them maintenance-free and leak-proof, as the gel prevents the acid from spilling even if the battery is tipped over. Gel Batteries are commonly used in applications where a clean and safe power source is needed, such as in solar power systems, telecommunications, medical equipment, and emergency lighting.</p><p>The Gel Batteries Market is witnessing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
