Some common types include needle roller thrust bearings, ball thrust bearings, and other specialized designs. Needle roller thrust bearings use cylindrical rollers to support high axial loads in a small space. Ball thrust bearings use spherical balls to support axial loads with low friction. Other types of plane thrust bearings may incorporate unique features or materials to address specific application requirements.</p></p>
