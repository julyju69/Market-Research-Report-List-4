<p><p>Stand alone inverters are categorized into two market types based on their output phases: Single Phase and Three Phase. Single Phase inverters are commonly used in residential settings to convert DC power from solar panels or batteries into AC power for appliances running on a single phase. Three Phase inverters are more commonly found in industrial or commercial applications where higher power outputs are required, converting DC power into AC power for machines running on three phase. These two market types cater to different needs and applications within the stand alone inverter market.</p></p>
