<p><p>Veterinary Infectious Disease Diagnostics market includes various types of diagnostic tests, with two commonly used methods being immunodiagnostics and ELISA. Immunodiagnostics involves the detection of antibodies or antigens in an animal's body to identify infectious diseases. ELISA, or enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay, is a specific type of immunodiagnostic test that detects antibodies or antigens through the use of enzymes and antibodies. Both methods play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring infectious diseases in animals, helping to ensure timely treatment and management.</p></p>
