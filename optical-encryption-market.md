<p><strong>The Optical Encryption Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTN or Layer 1</li><li>MACsec or Layer 2</li><li>IPsec or Layer 3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Encryption technology is categorized into three main types based on the layers at which encryption is performed. OTN or Layer 1 encryption involves securing data at the physical layer of the network, ensuring maximum security. MACsec or Layer 2 encryption focuses on securing data at the data link layer, providing enhanced protection for network traffic. IPsec or Layer 3 encryption secures data at the network layer, offering secure communication between network devices. Each type offers unique encryption capabilities tailored to specific network requirements.</p></p>
