<p><p>Amalgam carriers are dental instruments used to carry and dispense amalgam for dental fillings. There are two types of amalgam carriers: single and double. Single amalgam carriers have one compartment for carrying amalgam, while double carriers have two compartments, allowing for the use of two different types of amalgam. Both types are used by dentists to efficiently and accurately place amalgam in cavities during dental procedures. The choice between the two types depends on the dentist's preference and the specific needs of the procedure.</p></p>
