<p><p>Butter alternatives market includes two main types - Palm Oil Type and Lauric Oil Type. Palm Oil Type alternatives are derived from palm oil, have a similar consistency to butter, and are commonly used in baking and cooking. Lauric Oil Type alternatives, on the other hand, are derived from coconut or palm kernel oil, rich in lauric acid, and have a creamier texture. Both types are popular choices for individuals looking for dairy-free or vegan alternatives to butter.</p></p>
