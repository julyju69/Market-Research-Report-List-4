<p><p>BAW Filters are a type of acoustic wave filter used in various devices to eliminate unwanted frequencies and improve signal quality. There are two main types of BAW Filters: FBAR BAW Filters and SMR BAW Filters. FBAR BAW Filters use thin film bulk acoustic resonators to achieve high performance in a compact size. SMR BAW Filters, on the other hand, utilize solidly mounted resonators for improved stability and reliability. Both types of BAW Filters play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
