<p><p>The percutaneous nephroscope market includes two main types: rigid and flexible nephroscopes. Rigid nephroscopes are straight and have a fixed design, allowing for precise control and manipulation during procedures. They are commonly used for stone removal and other surgical interventions. Flexible nephroscopes have a bendable tip that enables access to hard-to-reach areas within the kidney, offering greater flexibility in navigation. Both types of nephroscopes play a crucial role in minimally invasive procedures for diagnosing and treating kidney disorders.</p></p>
