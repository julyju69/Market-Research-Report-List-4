<p><strong>The Rotary Ball Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ballscrew</li><li>External Circulation Ballscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary ball screws are classified into two main types based on their circulation system - Internal Circulation Ballscrew and External Circulation Ballscrew. Internal Circulation Ballscrew features a built-in circulation system within the nut and screw assembly, while External Circulation Ballscrew has an external tube for circulating the balls. Both types offer high precision and efficiency in converting rotary motion into linear motion. However, the choice between the two depends on factors such as load capacity, speed, and environmental conditions in which the ball screw will be operating.</p></p>
