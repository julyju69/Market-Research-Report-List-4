<p><p>RO Water Purifier market is divided into two main types based on installation and usage - Point of Use (POU) RO Water Purifiers and Point of Entry (POE) RO Water Purifiers. POU RO Water Purifiers are installed at the point where water is consumed, such as kitchen sink, providing purified water for drinking and cooking. On the other hand, POE RO Water Purifiers are installed at the main water supply line of the house, ensuring that all water coming into the house is purified.</p></p>
