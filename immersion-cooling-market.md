<p><p>Immersion cooling technology offers two main types of solutions: Single-Phase Immersion Cooling and Two-Phase Immersion Cooling. Single-Phase Immersion Cooling involves submerging IT equipment in a dielectric fluid to dissipate heat. This method is more straightforward and cost-effective. Two-Phase Immersion Cooling, on the other hand, uses a two-phase liquid, allowing for even more efficient heat transfer. This method is more complex but offers greater cooling capacity and performance, making it suitable for high-density and high-performance computing applications.</p></p>
