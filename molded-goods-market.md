<p><p>Molded goods market types include Liquid Injection Molding (LIM) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). LIM involves injecting liquid materials into a mold to create intricate shapes and designs. It is commonly used in manufacturing medical devices and consumer products. LSR, on the other hand, utilizes liquid silicone rubber to create durable and flexible products such as seals, gaskets, and medical implants. Both LIM and LSR processes offer benefits of high precision, fast production cycles, and cost-efficiency for producing molded goods.</p></p>
