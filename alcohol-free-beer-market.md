<p><p>Alcohol free beer market has two main types of production methods: fermentation-limited method and dealcoholization method. In the fermentation-limited method, the brewing process is altered to limit the production of alcohol, resulting in a product with very low alcohol content. On the other hand, in the dealcoholization method, alcohol is extracted from a fully brewed beer using various techniques such as reverse osmosis or vacuum distillation. Both methods result in alcohol free beer products that cater to consumers looking for non-alcoholic alternatives.</p></p>
