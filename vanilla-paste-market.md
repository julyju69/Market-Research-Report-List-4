<p><p>Vanilla Bean Paste (Single-Fold) is the most common type, with a one-to-one ratio of vanilla beans to extract, providing a balanced flavor profile. Vanilla Bean Paste (2-Fold) is more concentrated, containing twice the amount of vanilla beans as the single-fold version, resulting in a richer and more intense flavor. Vanilla Bean Paste (3-Fold) is the most concentrated option, with three times the amount of vanilla beans, offering a bold and robust vanilla taste. Each type caters to different preferences and applications in the culinary world.</p></p>
