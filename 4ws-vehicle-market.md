<p><ul><li>Mechanical 4WS</li><li>Hydraulic 4WS</li><li>Electro/Hydraulic 4WS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical 4WS vehicles use a mechanical linkage system to steer both the front and rear wheels for improved handling and maneuverability. Hydraulic 4WS vehicles utilize a hydraulic system to control the steering of all four wheels, providing better stability and control. Electro/Hydraulic 4WS vehicles combine electronic sensors with hydraulic actuators to steer the wheels, offering precise and adjustable steering performance. Each type of 4WS technology caters to different consumer needs and preferences in the market, ranging from enhanced driving dynamics to improved safety features.</p></p>
