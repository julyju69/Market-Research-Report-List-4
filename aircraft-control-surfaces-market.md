The main types of control surfaces include ailerons, which control the roll of the aircraft; elevators, used for pitch control; stabilators, which combine the functions of elevators and horizontal stabilizers; rudders, which control the yaw of the aircraft; and other surfaces such as flaps and spoilers that assist in controlling the aircraft in various flight conditions. Each control surface plays a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient flight operations.</p></p>
