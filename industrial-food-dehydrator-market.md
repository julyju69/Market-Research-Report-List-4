<p><strong>The Industrial Food Dehydrator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conduction Type</li><li>Convection Type</li><li>Radiation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial food dehydrators are categorized into three different types based on the method of heat transfer used: conduction type, convection type, and radiation type. Conduction type dehydrators transfer heat directly to the food through contact with a heated surface. Convection type dehydrators use hot air circulation to remove moisture from the food. Radiation type dehydrators utilize infrared radiation to heat and dehydrate the food. Each type offers different benefits and is suitable for different industrial food dehydration processes.</p></p>
