<p><p>Power cable materials market consists of insulation materials and jacket materials. Insulation materials are used to provide electrical insulation between conductive materials in the cable, ensuring safety and reliability. Common insulation materials include polyvinyl chloride (PVC), cross-linked polyethylene (XLPE), and rubber. Jacket materials are used to protect the cable from environmental factors like moisture, chemicals, and physical damage. Common jacket materials include PVC, polyethylene, and chlorosulfonated polyethylene (CSPE). Both insulation and jacket materials play a crucial role in the performance and longevity of power cables.</p></p>
