<p><p>Industrial cordsets market can be categorized into two types: Single-Ended Cordsets and Double-Ended Cordsets. Single-Ended Cordsets have a connector on one end and loose wires on the other end, making them suitable for applications where customization is required. On the other hand, Double-Ended Cordsets have connectors on both ends, providing a plug-and-play solution for quick and easy installation. Both types offer flexibility and convenience in industrial settings, catering to different needs and preferences of users.</p></p>
