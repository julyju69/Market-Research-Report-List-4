<p><p>Commercial dough sheeters are available in two main types: countertop and floor models. Countertop dough sheeters are compact and are best suited for small bakeries or kitchens with limited space. They are designed to sit on top of a countertop and are easy to move around. Floor models are larger and more heavy-duty, making them ideal for high-volume bakeries or food production facilities. They are designed to be placed directly on the floor and can handle larger batches of dough.</p></p>
