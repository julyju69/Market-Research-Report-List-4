<p><p>Polyol sweeteners are sugar alcohols that are commonly used as sugar substitutes in various food and beverage products. The main types of polyol sweeteners include sorbitol, erythritol, maltitol, isomalt, and xylitol. Sorbitol is often used in sugar-free candies and chewing gum, while erythritol is a low-calorie sweetener with zero net carbohydrates. Maltitol is commonly found in sugar-free chocolates, while isomalt is used in bakery products. Xylitol is known for its dental benefits and is often used in sugar-free gum and mints.</p></p>
