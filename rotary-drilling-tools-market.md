Augers are used for drilling through soil and rock, while drilling buckets are specifically designed for excavating larger diameter holes. Core barrels extract core samples from the ground for analysis, and temporary casing is used to support the hole while drilling and to protect the surrounding environment. These tools play a crucial role in various drilling applications across industries such as construction, mining, and oil & gas.</p></p>
