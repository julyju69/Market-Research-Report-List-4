<p><strong>The Organ Preservation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Cold Storage</li><li>Hypothermic Machine Perfusion</li><li>Normothermic Machine Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organ preservation market includes three main types: static cold storage, hypothermic machine perfusion, and normothermic machine perfusion. Static cold storage involves storing organs in simple cold containers until transplantation. Hypothermic machine perfusion involves circulating cold preservation solution through the organ to maintain its viability. Normothermic machine perfusion maintains the organ at body temperature and provides nutrients and oxygen, mimicking conditions in the human body. Each method has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different organ preservation needs.</p></p>
