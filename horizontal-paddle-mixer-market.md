<p><p>Horizontal paddle mixers are commonly categorized into two market types: continuous and discontinuous. Continuous paddle mixers are designed to mix materials in a constant flow, allowing for a continuous production process. On the other hand, discontinuous paddle mixers are used for batch mixing, where materials are mixed in separate batches. Both types of mixers offer unique advantages depending on the production requirements of the user, such as efficiency and flexibility in workflow.</p></p>
