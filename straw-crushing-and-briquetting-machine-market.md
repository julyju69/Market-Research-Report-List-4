<p><p>Straw crushing and briquetting machines are available in three main market types: Mechanical, Hydraulic, and Screw Extrusion. Mechanical machines use a rotating mechanism to crush and compress straw into briquettes. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic power to apply pressure and compress the straw into briquettes. Screw Extrusion machines use a screw mechanism to crush and compress the straw. Each market type offers unique advantages and capabilities, catering to different needs and preferences within the industry.</p></p>
