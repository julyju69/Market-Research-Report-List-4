<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) Fibers Market consists of two types - Continuous Silicon Carbide (SiC) Fibers and Silicon Carbide Short Fibers. Continuous SiC fibers are long, continuous strands of SiC with high tensile strength and superior thermal conductivity, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and high performance. On the other hand, SiC short fibers are shorter in length and offer good tensile strength and chemical resistance, suitable for use in composite materials to enhance mechanical properties. Both types play a key role in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and energy.</p></p>
