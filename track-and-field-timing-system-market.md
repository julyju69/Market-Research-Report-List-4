Line-scan Systems utilize cameras to capture athletes crossing a finish line. Full-frame Systems capture entire frames for precise timing. Break-beam Timing Systems use sensors to detect when athletes break a beam of light. Each system offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, ease of use, and cost.
