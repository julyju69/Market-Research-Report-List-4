<p><p>Single-use filtration assemblies consist of various types in the market. Membrane filtration assemblies are used for separating particles based on size, while depth filtration assemblies use a porous material to trap particles within the filter matrix. Centrifugation assemblies separate substances based on their densities using centrifugal force. Other types of single-use filtration assemblies may include technologies such as ultrafiltration or nanofiltration, each with their own specific applications in biopharmaceutical, food and beverage, and other industries.</p></p>
