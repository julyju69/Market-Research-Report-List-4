<p><p>Defence life jacket market includes three main types: foam jacket, inflatable jacket, and hybrid jacket. Foam jackets are made with buoyant foam material, providing constant flotation without the need for inflation. Inflatable jackets feature chambers that can be filled with air manually or automatically, offering comfort and mobility. Hybrid jackets combine foam and inflatable technology for enhanced buoyancy and versatility. Each type caters to different preferences and needs, ensuring individuals can choose the best option for their safety and comfort in water-related activities.</p></p>
