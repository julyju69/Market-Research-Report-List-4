<p><p>Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is a type of polyethylene with a linear molecular structure, offering superior strength and puncture resistance compared to traditional low-density polyethylene. The market is segmented based on the type of comonomer used in its production - C4- LLDPE, C6- LLDPE, and C8- LLDPE. C4- LLDPE is copolymerized with butene, C6- LLDPE with hexene, and C8- LLDPE with octene. Each type offers unique properties and is used in various applications such as packaging films, industrial liners, and agricultural films.</p></p>
