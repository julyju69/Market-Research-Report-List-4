<p><p>Sliced cheese is available in two main market types: processed cheese and natural cheese. Processed cheese is made from blending natural cheese with emulsifiers, stabilizers, and flavoring agents. It is known for its smooth texture and long shelf life. On the other hand, natural cheese is made from milk, salt, and enzymes, without any additional ingredients. It is appreciated for its rich flavor and versatility in cooking. Both types of sliced cheese cater to different consumer preferences and usage occasions in the market.</p></p>
