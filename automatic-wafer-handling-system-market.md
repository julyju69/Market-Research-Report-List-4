<p><p>Automatic wafer handling systems are divided into two main types based on the material of the boat used for carrying wafers - quartz boat and graphite boat market. Quartz boats are preferred for their superior purity and resistance to thermal shock, making them suitable for high-temperature processes. On the other hand, graphite boats are known for their cost-effectiveness and thermal conductivity, making them a popular choice for low-temperature processes. Both types of boats play a crucial role in the efficient handling and processing of wafers in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
