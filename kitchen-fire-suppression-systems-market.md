<p><p>Kitchen fire suppression systems come in two main types: manual trigger and thermal trigger. Manual trigger systems require a person to manually activate the system in case of a fire emergency, while thermal trigger systems automatically detect high temperatures and release the extinguishing agent. Manual trigger systems offer more control to the user, while thermal trigger systems provide a more automatic solution for fire suppression. Both systems are crucial for ensuring safety in commercial kitchen environments and are designed to quickly and effectively extinguish fires.</p></p>
