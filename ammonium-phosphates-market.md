<p><p>Ammonium Phosphates market is segmented into three main types: Mono-Ammonium Phosphate (MAP), Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP), and Ammonium Polyphosphate. MAP is a highly concentrated fertilizer containing nitrogen and phosphate, suitable for soils with pH levels above 7. DAP is a common phosphate fertilizer known for its high content of nitrogen and phosphorus, ideal for acidic soils. Ammonium Polyphosphate is a liquid fertilizer with slow-release properties, providing a steady nutrient supply to crops over time.</p></p>
