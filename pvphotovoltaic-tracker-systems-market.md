<p><strong>The PV(Photovoltaic) Tracker Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Axis Trackers</li><li>Dual Axis Trackers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PV Tracker Systems are used to increase the efficiency of solar panels by tracking the movement of the sun. Single Axis Trackers move panels along one axis to follow the sun's east-west path, maximizing energy generation. Dual Axis Trackers move panels along two axes to track both the east-west path and the sun's vertical movement, further optimizing energy production. While Single Axis Trackers are more common due to their lower cost, Dual Axis Trackers offer superior performance and are often utilized in areas with higher energy demands.</p></p>
