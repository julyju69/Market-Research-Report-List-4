Polyacrylate hydrogels are known for their high water absorbency and flexibility. Polyacrylamide hydrogels are commonly used in biomedical applications due to their biocompatibility. Silicon hydrogels are known for their high oxygen permeability and are widely used in contact lenses. Other types of hydrogels include natural polymers like alginate and chitosan, as well as synthetic polymers such as polylactide and polyethylene glycol.</p></p>
