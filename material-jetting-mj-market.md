<p><p>Material Jetting (MJ) is a 3D printing technology that uses a print head to selectively deposit material layer by layer. Polymer Jetting is a type of MJ that focuses on printing with polymers, allowing for high-resolution and detailed objects to be produced. Metal Jetting, on the other hand, uses metal powders as the material to create parts with high mechanical properties and excellent surface finish. Both Polymer Jetting and Metal Jetting markets are growing as demand for customized and complex parts increases in various industries.</p></p>
