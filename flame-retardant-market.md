<p><p>The flame retardant market can be classified into different types based on the chemical compounds used. Decabromodiphenyl Ether, Brominated Epoxy Resin, and Brominated Polystyrene are examples of halogenated flame retardants, which are widely used for their efficient fire suppression properties. Halogen-free flame retardants are gaining popularity due to concerns about the environmental and health impacts of halogenated compounds. Other types of flame retardants include phosphorus-based compounds, aluminum hydroxide, and magnesium hydroxide, which offer different mechanisms of flame suppression and are used in various applications.</p></p>
