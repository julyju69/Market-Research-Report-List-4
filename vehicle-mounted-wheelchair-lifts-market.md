<p><p>Vehicle mounted wheelchair lifts are available in three main types: Inside Lift, Outside Lift, and Hybrid Lift.</p><p>Inside Lifts are installed inside the vehicle and are typically mounted in the trunk. They are commonly used in vans and SUVs.</p><p>Outside Lifts are mounted outside the vehicle and typically on the back. They are favored for their ease of use and ability to lift heavy wheelchairs.</p><p>Hybrid Lifts combine features of both inside and outside lifts, offering versatility and convenience for wheelchair users.</p></p>
