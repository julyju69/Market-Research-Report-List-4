<p><p>The insulin patch pump market can be categorized into three types based on their functions: Basal Insulin, Bolus Insulin, and Basal-Bolus Insulin. Basal Insulin provides a continuous low dose of insulin to mimic the body's basal insulin production. Bolus Insulin delivers a higher dose of insulin at meal times to control blood sugar spikes. Basal-Bolus Insulin combines both basal and bolus insulin delivery to provide more precise and personalized insulin dosing for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
