<p><p>Flat panel display market is dominated by three main types: Organic Light Emitting Diode Display (OLED), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Plasma Display (PDP). OLED displays offer vivid colors, high contrast ratios, and energy efficiency but are expensive. LCD displays are affordable, energy-efficient, and come in a variety of sizes but have limited viewing angles. PDP displays deliver impressive image quality and are commonly used in larger screen sizes, but they consume more power and are bulkier compared to OLED and LCD displays.</p></p>
