<p><p>Aluminium powder market is segmented into two main types: non-spherical aluminum powder and spherical aluminum powder. Non-spherical aluminum powder consists of irregularly shaped particles and is commonly used in applications where flowability is a key factor. On the other hand, spherical aluminum powder is composed of uniformly round particles, which provide better flow properties and are often used in industries where high-quality finished products are required. Both types of aluminum powder play a crucial role in various industrial processes and applications.</p></p>
