<p><p>Prebiotic food market types include functional oligosaccharides, which are non-digestible carbohydrates that promote the growth of beneficial bacteria in the gut. Polysaccharides are complex carbohydrates that provide a food source for probiotic bacteria. Polyols, also known as sugar alcohols, are another type of prebiotic that can aid in digestion and improve gut health. Other prebiotic foods include resistant starches, which resist digestion and act as a food source for beneficial gut bacteria.</p></p>
