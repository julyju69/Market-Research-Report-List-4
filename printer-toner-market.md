<p><ul><li>Conventional Toner</li><li>Chemically Prepared Toner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printer toner market is segmented into two main types: Conventional toner and Chemically Prepared toner. Conventional toner is made using a mechanical process which involves grinding and crushing raw materials. This type of toner is affordable but may produce lower quality prints. Chemically Prepared toner, on the other hand, is manufactured using a complex chemical process that results in higher quality prints with improved color accuracy and consistency. This type of toner is more expensive but provides better results for professional printing needs.</p></p>
