<p><p>Spoolable pipe market is categorized into three main types based on reinforcement material: fiber reinforced, steel reinforced, and hybrid reinforcement. Fiber reinforced pipes are made with high-strength fibers such as carbon or glass, providing flexibility and corrosion resistance. Steel reinforced pipes have a steel layer for added strength and are suitable for high-pressure applications. Hybrid reinforcement pipes combine both fiber and steel materials to offer a balance of strength and flexibility, making them versatile for various industrial uses.</p></p>
