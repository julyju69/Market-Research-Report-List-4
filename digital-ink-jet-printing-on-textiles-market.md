Direct-to-Garment Printing involves printing directly onto garments, while Direct-to-Fabric Printing is used for printing onto fabric before garment assembly. Dye Sublimation Printing involves transferring dye onto fabric using heat, and Care Label Printing is used for printing care instructions on textiles. Each of these market types serves a specific purpose in the textile printing industry.</p></p>
