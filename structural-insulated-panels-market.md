<p><p>Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs) are high-performance building panels used in walls, floors, and roofs for residential and commercial buildings. They consist of an insulating foam core sandwiched between two structural facings, typically made of oriented strand board (OSB). SIPs are known for their energy efficiency, strength, and quick installation, making them a popular choice in the construction industry.</p><p>The Structural Insulated Panels Market is witnessing significant growth, with a CAGR of 7.20% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for sustainable and energy-efficient construction materials is driving the market growth. SIPs provide better insulation compared to traditional building materials, resulting in reduced energy consumption and lower utility bills for homeowners. Additionally, SIPs help in faster construction, reducing labor costs and overall project timelines.</p><p>Key trends in the Structural Insulated Panels Market include the growing adoption of green building practices, government initiatives promoting energy-efficient construction, and technological advancements leading to the development of high-performance SIPs. <p><p>Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs) market can be divided into four main types based on the core material used in the panels - EPS Panels, PUR/PIR Panels, Glass Wool Panels, and Others. EPS Panels are made from expanded polystyrene foam, PUR/PIR Panels from polyurethane or polyisocyanurate foam, Glass Wool Panels from glass fibers, and Other types can include materials like extruded polystyrene or mineral wool. Each type of panel offers different levels of insulation, strength, and cost-effectiveness for various construction applications.</p></p>
