<p><p>Biomarker testing is the process of analyzing biological markers in the body that can indicate the presence of a particular disease or condition. These biomarkers can be found in blood, urine, tissues, or other bodily fluids and are used to diagnose, monitor, and predict disease progression. Biomarker testing is crucial for personalized medicine as it helps in identifying the most effective treatment options for patients based on their individual genetic makeup and disease profile.</p><p>The Biomarker Testing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in technology such as next-generation sequencing, digital PCR, and immunohistochemistry have revolutionized biomarker testing, leading to more accurate and reliable results.</p><p>Moreover, the rising demand for personalized medicine and targeted therapy is driving the growth of the biomarker testing market. Companies are investing in research and development to discover novel biomarkers and develop innovative diagnostic tools to cater to the growing demand for precision medicine.
