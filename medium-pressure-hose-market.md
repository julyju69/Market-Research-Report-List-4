<p><p>Medium pressure hose market can be categorized into two main types: fabric reinforced and steel wire reinforced hoses. Fabric reinforced hoses are constructed with layers of fabric material such as nylon or polyester, providing flexibility and durability. On the other hand, steel wire reinforced hoses have metal wire braiding or spiral layers for added strength and resistance to high pressure applications. Both types of hoses are commonly used in various industries for conveying fluids and are chosen based on specific requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
