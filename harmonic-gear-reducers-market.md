<p><p>Harmonic gear reducers come in various types, including cup style, hat style, and pancake style. Cup style reducers feature a circular cup-shaped spline that houses the flexspline and circular spline. Hat style reducers have a hat-shaped flexspline and circular spline, providing higher torque capacity. Pancake style reducers have a flat design with a flexspline and circular spline stacked on top of each other. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of torque capacity, compactness, and efficiency, catering to different industrial applications.</p></p>
