<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Corrugated Fiberboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-flute</li><li>B-flute</li><li>C-flute</li><li>E-flute</li><li>F-flute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated fiberboard is categorized into different types based on the size and shape of the flutes, which are the accordion-like ridges that give the material its strength and flexibility. A-flute is the largest and thickest, providing excellent cushioning and stacking strength. B-flute is smaller and offers good printing surface. C-flute is the most common, providing a balance between cushioning and stacking strength. E-flute is thin and provides a smooth surface for printing. F-flute is the thinnest and most lightweight option, suitable for high-end packaging.</p></p>
