<p><p>Cold work die steel is used for various applications in the manufacturing industry. Blanking dies are used to cut sheet metal into specific shapes, while cold upsetting dies are used to form metal into desired shapes through compression. Cold extrusion dies are used to create parts with complex shapes by forcing metal through a die. Bending dies are used to bend metal sheets or tubes, while drawing dies are used to draw metal into wire or tube shapes. Each type of die steel serves a specific purpose in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
