<p><p>Carbon-ceramic discs are high-performance braking systems used in luxury and sports cars. The two main types of carbon-ceramic discs available in the market are drilled and slotted discs. Drilled carbon-ceramic discs have holes drilled into them to dissipate heat and improve braking performance. On the other hand, slotted carbon-ceramic discs have slots cut into them to prevent brake fade and maintain consistent braking performance. Both types offer superior braking capabilities compared to traditional brake discs.</p></p>
