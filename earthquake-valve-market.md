<p><p>Earthquake Valve Market Types include Motion Sensing Caged Ball and Excessive Flow Sensor valves. Motion Sensing Caged Ball valves are designed to automatically shut off gas flow when they sense motion caused by an earthquake, providing protection against gas leaks and potential fires. Excessive Flow Sensor valves, on the other hand, monitor gas flow rates and shut off supply if they detect an abnormal increase, preventing gas leaks and reducing the risk of explosions. Both types of valves enhance safety and reduce the impact of natural disasters.</p></p>
