<p><p>Arc welding equipment market can be categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. In manual welding, the welder has full control over the welding process, which allows for more precision but requires highly skilled labor. Semi-automatic welding involves a combination of manual and automatic processes, making it easier for operators to use. Fully automatic welding systems are completely automated, requiring minimal human intervention, making them efficient for high-volume production. Each type of welding equipment offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
