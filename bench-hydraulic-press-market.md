<p><p>H-Type and C-Type hydraulic presses are two common types of bench hydraulic presses used in various industries. H-Type hydraulic press has a horizontal ram with a fixed bed and is suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, C-Type hydraulic press has a vertical ram and bed, making it more compact and versatile for lighter applications. Both types are used for tasks such as bending, shaping, and pressing metal or other materials, with each having its own advantages depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
