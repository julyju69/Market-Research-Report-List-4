<p><p>uPVC windows are available in three main types based on the number of glass panes used: Single glazing, double glazing, and triple glazing. Single glazing windows consist of a single pane of glass, offering basic insulation. Double glazing windows have two glass panes with a space between them, providing better insulation and soundproofing. Triple glazing windows have three glass panes, offering the highest level of insulation and noise reduction. Each type caters to different needs and requirements, with triple glazing being the most energy-efficient option.</p></p>
