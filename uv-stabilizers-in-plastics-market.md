<p><p>UV stabilizers are additives used in plastics to protect them from degradation caused by exposure to ultraviolet (UV) radiation. There are several types of UV stabilizers in the market including Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), UV absorbers, Quenchers, and others. HALS work by scavenging free radicals formed during UV exposure, UV absorbers absorb UV radiation and convert it into heat, Quenchers work by deactivating excited states of molecules generated by UV light, and other stabilizers function through various mechanisms to protect plastics from UV-induced damage.</p></p>
