<p><strong>The API Tanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Roof</li><li>External Floating Roof</li><li>Internal Floating Roof</li><li>Dome External Floating Roof</li></ul></p>
<p><p>API tanks can be categorized into different market types based on their roof structures. Fixed roof tanks have a permanently attached roof, while external floating roof tanks have a roof that floats on the surface of the liquid inside the tank. Internal floating roof tanks have a roof that floats directly on the liquid, reducing vapor space. Dome external floating roof tanks have a dome-shaped roof that floats on the liquid surface, providing additional protection against vapor emissions. Each tank type offers unique advantages and is suited for different industry applications.</p></p>
