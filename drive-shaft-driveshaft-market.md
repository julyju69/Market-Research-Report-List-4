<p><p>Drive shaft market is segmented into two types- Half Shaft and Propeller Shaft. Half Shaft is a type of drive shaft used in front-wheel drive vehicles, transferring power from the transmission to the wheels. Propeller Shaft, on the other hand, is used in rear-wheel drive vehicles, transmitting power from the transmission to the rear differential. Both types of drive shafts play a crucial role in transferring power from the engine to the wheels, allowing the vehicle to move efficiently.</p></p>
