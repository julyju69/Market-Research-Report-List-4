<p><p>Automatic Flexographic Printer market consists of two main types: Unit-type machines and Central Impression machines. Unit-type machines are smaller, more compact printers typically used for printing on smaller substrates like labels and packaging materials. Central Impression machines are larger, more complex printers designed for printing on larger, continuous rolls of materials like newspapers and corrugated cardboard. Both types of machines are used in various industries for high-speed, high-quality printing of images and text onto different types of materials.</p></p>
