<p><p>Bronze ball valves are commonly categorized into two main types: manually operated and automatic. Manually operated bronze ball valves require a person to physically turn a handle to control the flow of fluid, while automatic bronze ball valves use sensors or actuators to regulate flow without human intervention. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific application, with manually operated valves providing simplicity and control for smaller systems, while automatic valves offer increased efficiency and automation for larger or complex systems.</p></p>
