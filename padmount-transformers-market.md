<p><p>Padmount Transformers are used to step down high voltage electricity for distribution to lower voltage loads. There are two main types of Padmount Transformers based on the insulation material used inside the transformer: Dry-type Padmount Transformers and Liquid-immersed Padmount Transformers. Dry-type transformers use solid insulation materials such as epoxy resin, while Liquid-immersed transformers use oil or other liquid materials for insulation. Both types have different applications based on factors such as safety, efficiency, and environmental concerns.</p></p>
