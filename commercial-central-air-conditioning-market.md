<p><p>The commercial central air conditioning market includes different types such as water-cooled air conditioning, multi-split packaged air conditioning, single-split packaged air conditioning, and others. Water-cooled air conditioning systems use water as a cooling medium. Multi-split packaged ACs have multiple indoor units connected to one outdoor unit. Single-split packaged ACs have one indoor unit connected to one outdoor unit. Other types of central air conditioning include variable refrigerant flow (VRF) systems, ductless mini-split systems, and rooftop packaged units. Each type offers unique advantages for commercial cooling needs.</p></p>
