<p><p>High Performance Pigments (HPP) can be classified into two main categories: Department of Organic HPP and Department of Inorganic HPP. Organic HPPs are derived from carbon-based compounds and are known for their vibrant and lightfast colors. They are widely used in industries such as plastics, paints, and coatings. On the other hand, inorganic HPPs are composed of metal oxides and are valued for their high opacity and durability. They are commonly used in applications requiring heat resistance and chemical stability.</p></p>
