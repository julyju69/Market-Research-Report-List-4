<p><p>Peptic ulcer drugs market types include Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) which reduce stomach acid production, H2-Antagonists which block histamine receptors to decrease acid secretion, Antacids which neutralize stomach acid, and others such as prostaglandin analogs or mucosal protectants. PPIs are the most commonly prescribed drugs for peptic ulcers due to their effectiveness in reducing acid levels. H2-Antagonists are also widely used, while antacids provide quick relief for symptoms. Other drugs may be prescribed depending on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
