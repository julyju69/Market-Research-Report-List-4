<p><p>CMOS and CCD are two types of image sensors used in smart cameras. CMOS sensors are more popular due to their low power consumption, fast data readout, and lower manufacturing costs. Both sensors have their own advantages and limitations, and their market share in smart cameras depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
