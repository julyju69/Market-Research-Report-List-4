<p><p>Wood restorers are products used to rejuvenate and protect wooden surfaces. There are two main types of wood restorers on the market: water-based and oil-based. Water-based wood restorers are eco-friendly, easy to clean up, and dry quickly. They are ideal for indoor use and are low in toxicity. Oil-based wood restorers, on the other hand, provide a durable finish and are more resistant to wear and tear. They are commonly used for outdoor wooden surfaces due to their ability to penetrate deeper into the wood and provide long-lasting protection.</p></p>
