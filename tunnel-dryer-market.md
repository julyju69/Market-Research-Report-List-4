<p><p>Tunnel dryers are classified into two main types: Solar Type and Blower Type. Solar Type tunnel dryers utilize solar energy to heat the air inside the drying unit, reducing energy costs and environmental impact. On the other hand, Blower Type tunnel dryers rely on electric blowers to circulate heated air, offering more consistent drying temperatures and higher efficiency. Both types are widely used in the food processing, agricultural, and industrial sectors to efficiently dry various products such as fruits, vegetables, grains, and chemicals.</p></p>
