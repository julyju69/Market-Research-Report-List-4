PUR cables are known for their durability and resistance to abrasion, making them ideal for harsh environments. PVC cables are cost-effective and flexible, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. TPE cables offer a balance between durability and flexibility, making them a popular choice for various industries. Each material has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
