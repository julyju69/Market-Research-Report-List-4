<p><ul><li>CMP Slurry</li><li>CMP Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) polishing material market is divided into two main types - CMP Slurry and CMP Pad. CMP Slurry is a mixture of abrasive particles and chemicals used to remove material during the polishing process. It plays a crucial role in achieving the desired surface finish. On the other hand, CMP Pad is a soft material used as a polishing surface to evenly distribute pressure and abrasive particles during the polishing process. Both CMP Slurry and CMP Pad are essential components in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
