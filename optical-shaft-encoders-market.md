<p><p>Optical Shaft Encoders are devices used to convert the angular position or motion of a shaft into an analog or digital signal. The market for these encoders can be classified into two types - Incremental Shaft Encoders and Absolute Shaft Encoders. </p><p>Incremental Shaft Encoders provide relative position information by generating pulses for each incremental movement of the shaft. Absolute Shaft Encoders, on the other hand, provide absolute position information by assigning a unique digital code to each position of the shaft. Both types of encoders find applications in various industries such as robotics, automation, and manufacturing.</p></p>
