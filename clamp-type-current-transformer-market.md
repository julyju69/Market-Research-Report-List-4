<p><p>Clamp Type Current Transformers come in various types based on the turns ratio. These include Turns Ratio 1:1000, Turns Ratio 1:2000, Turns Ratio 1:2500, and others. The Turns Ratio indicates the relationship between the primary current and secondary current. For example, a Turns Ratio of 1:1000 means that for every 1 ampere of current in the primary winding, there will be 1000 amperes in the secondary winding. Different turns ratios are used based on the specific application and requirements of the electrical system.</p></p>
