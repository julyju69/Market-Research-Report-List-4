<p><ul><li>Toothless Type</li><li>External Type</li><li>Internal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swing bearings are mainly classified into three types: Toothless Type, External Type, and Internal Type. Toothless Type swing bearings are built without teeth and are commonly used in applications where a high degree of precision is required. External Type swing bearings have external gears attached to them, allowing for more strength and durability. Internal Type swing bearings have internal gears, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
