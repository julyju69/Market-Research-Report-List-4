<p><p>Wet Fire Sprinkler Systems consist of various components such as sprinkler heads, piping systems, wet alarm valves, alarm devices, and water supply systems. Sprinkler heads are devices that discharge water when triggered by high temperatures. Piping systems distribute water throughout the building. Wet alarm valves control the flow of water to the sprinkler heads. Alarm devices alert occupants of a fire emergency. Water supply systems provide the necessary water for the sprinkler system to function effectively. These components work together to ensure quick and efficient fire suppression in buildings.</p></p>
