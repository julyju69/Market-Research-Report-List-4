<p><p>Fireplace glass market consists of two main types: ceramic glass and tempered glass. Ceramic glass is a type of heat-resistant glass that can withstand high temperatures and is often used in fireplaces due to its ability to handle direct contact with flames. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is specially treated for increased strength and safety. It is shatter-resistant and designed to withstand impact and thermal stress, making it a popular choice for fireplace doors and inserts. Both types of glass offer different advantages and cater to varying needs in the fireplace market.</p></p>
