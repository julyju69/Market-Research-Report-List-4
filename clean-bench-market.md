<p><strong>The Clean Bench Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Flow Clean Bench</li><li>Horizontal Flow Clean Bench</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vertical flow clean benches are used in environments where protection from airborne contaminants is needed, such as in laboratories and medical facilities. They provide a continuous flow of clean air from the top of the bench to the work surface, ensuring a sterile working area. Horizontal flow clean benches, on the other hand, are commonly used in industries such as electronics and pharmaceuticals, where laminar airflow is required to protect sensitive materials from contamination. Both types of clean benches help maintain a clean and controlled working environment.</p></p>
