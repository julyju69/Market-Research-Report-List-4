<p><p>Electro-pneumatic regulators are divided into two main types based on their control functions: flow control and pressure control. Flow control regulators are used to adjust the flow rate of gases or liquids in a system, while pressure control regulators are utilized to maintain a constant pressure level within a system. These two types of regulators play crucial roles in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, where precise control of flow and pressure is essential for efficient operation.</p></p>
