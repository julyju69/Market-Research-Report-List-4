<p><strong>The Robotic Palletizing Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monobloc</li><li>Compact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Robotic palletizing cells come in two main types: Monobloc and Compact. Monobloc palletizing cells are characterized by their integrated design, where all components such as the robot, palletizer, and conveyor system are housed in a single structure. This allows for a seamless and compact layout, making them ideal for limited space environments. On the other hand, Compact palletizing cells feature a modular design, where each component is separate but can be easily integrated together. This offers flexibility in customizing the layout to suit specific requirements.</p></p>
