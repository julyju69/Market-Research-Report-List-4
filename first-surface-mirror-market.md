<p><ul><li>Glass Substrates</li><li>Metallic Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>First Surface Mirror market includes two main types based on the substrate material used: Glass Substrates and Metallic Substrates. Glass Substrates mirrors are made by depositing a thin layer of reflective material such as aluminum or silver directly onto the glass surface, resulting in high optical quality and durability. Meanwhile, Metallic Substrates mirrors are manufactured by coating a metal substrate with a reflective layer, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to different industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
