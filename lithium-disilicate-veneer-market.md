<p><p>Lithium disilicate veneers are categorized based on their flexural strength. Veneers with a strength of 300-400 MPa are moderate strength, offering durability for most patients. Veneers with a strength exceeding 400 MPa are high strength, recommended for patients with heavy occlusal forces or bruxism. Dentists can choose veneers based on the patient's specific needs and expected wear and tear.</p></p>
