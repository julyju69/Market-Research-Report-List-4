<p><strong>The Solar Microgrid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied</li><li>Independent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grid-tied solar microgrids are connected to the main utility grid and can sell excess electricity back to the grid, providing a reliable source of renewable energy and potential cost savings for consumers. Independent solar microgrids operate off-grid and are self-sustaining, often used in remote areas or during power outages. They rely solely on solar energy storage and backup generators, providing reliable power without the need for a connection to the main utility grid. Both types offer sustainable and reliable energy solutions with different levels of grid connectivity.</p></p>
