<p><p>Sugar syrup market includes various types such as Glucose Syrup, Corn Syrup, Maple Syrup, Cane Syrup, and others. Glucose syrup, derived from corn starch, is widely used in food and beverage production. Corn syrup is similar to glucose syrup but made from corn. Maple syrup is made from the sap of maple trees. Cane syrup is a sweet syrup made from sugarcane juice. Other types of sugar syrups include agave syrup, honey, and fruit syrups. Each type has unique characteristics and uses in the food industry.</p></p>
