<p><p>Front terminal batteries are widely used in various applications. The two main types in the market are AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and GEL batteries. AGM batteries use a fiberglass mat to suspend the electrolyte, making them more durable and resistant to vibration. GEL batteries, on the other hand, use a silica gel to immobilize the electrolyte, providing a higher level of safety and maintenance-free operation. Both types are known for their reliable performance and long service life in front terminal applications.</p></p>
