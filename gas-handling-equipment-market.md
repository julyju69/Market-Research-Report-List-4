<p><p>Gas handling equipment market can be categorized into four main types: Generation, Storage, Detection, and Transportation. Generation equipment includes devices like gas generators that produce gas for various industrial applications. Storage equipment involves tanks and cylinders that store gases safely. Detection equipment includes sensors and monitors that detect gas leaks or levels. Transportation equipment includes pipelines and compressors that transport gases from one location to another safely and efficiently. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the safe handling and usage of gases in various industries.</p></p>
