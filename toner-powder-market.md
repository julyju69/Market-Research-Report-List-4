<p><ul><li>Physical Toner</li><li>Chemical Toner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physical toner and chemical toner are two types of toner powders used in the printing industry. Physical toner is made from tiny particles of plastic and pigment that are fused onto paper using heat during the printing process. It is commonly used in laser printers and photocopiers. On the other hand, chemical toner is made from a complex combination of chemicals that react with paper to produce high-quality prints. Both types of toner have their own benefits and are used in different printing applications.</p></p>
