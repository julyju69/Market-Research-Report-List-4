<p><p>Darkroom lamps are specialized lamps used in photography darkrooms to provide dim lighting that does not affect light-sensitive materials. They come in various colors, including red, white, and blue. Red darkroom lamps are commonly used for developing black and white prints, as they have the least impact on photo-sensitive materials. White darkroom lamps are used for general lighting purposes, while blue darkroom lamps are popular for alternative processes. Other types of darkroom lamps may include multi-colored or adjustable intensity lamps to meet different darkroom needs.</p></p>
