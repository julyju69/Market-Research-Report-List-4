<p><p>Generator protection relays are available in three main market types: DIN Installation, Panel Installation, and Embedded. DIN Installation relays are designed to be mounted on a standard DIN rail within a control panel. Panel Installation relays are directly mounted on the control panel for easy access and visibility. Embedded relays are integrated within the generator unit itself, offering a compact and space-saving solution. Each market type caters to different installation requirements, providing flexibility and convenience for various generator protection applications.</p></p>
