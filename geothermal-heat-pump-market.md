<p><p>Geothermal heat pumps come in different types depending on the installation method of their ground heat exchanger system. The four main market types are Vertical Closed Loop, Horizontal Closed Loop, Open Loop, and Others. Vertical Closed Loop systems have pipes drilled deep into the ground, Horizontal Closed Loop systems are buried in shallow trenches, and Open Loop systems use water from a well or body of water for heat exchange. Other types may include pond loops, hybrid systems, or direct exchange setups. Each type has its own benefits and considerations for different applications.</p></p>
