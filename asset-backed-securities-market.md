<p><p>Existing Assets market for Asset-backed Securities involves the issuance of securities that are backed by physical assets such as real estate or equipment. These securities derive their value from the underlying assets and provide investors with a claim on the cash flows generated by those assets. On the other hand, Future Cash Flow market for Asset-backed Securities involves securities that are backed by the future cash flows from a pool of assets, such as loans or receivables. Investors in this market receive payments based on the cash flows generated by the underlying assets.</p></p>
