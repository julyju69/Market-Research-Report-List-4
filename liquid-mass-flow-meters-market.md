<p><p>Liquid mass flow meters are used in closed pipe and open channel applications to measure the flow rate of liquids. Closed pipe flow meters are installed within a closed conduit, such as a pipe, and measure the flow of liquid through the pipe. Open channel flow meters, on the other hand, are used in applications where the liquid flows freely in an open channel, such as rivers or streams. Both types of flow meters are essential in accurately measuring and monitoring liquid flow in various industrial and environmental applications.</p></p>
