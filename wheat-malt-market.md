<p><p>Wheat malt market primarily consists of two main types - White Malt and Red Malt. White malt is produced from wheat grains that have been dried and kilned at low temperatures, resulting in a light color and mild flavor profile. On the other hand, Red Malt is made by roasting wheat grains at higher temperatures, giving it a darker color and richer taste. Both types of malt are commonly used in brewing beer, distilling spirits, and baking to add unique flavors and aromas to the final product.</p></p>
