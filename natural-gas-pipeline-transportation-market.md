<p><p>Natural gas pipeline transportation market consists of various types such as gathering pipelines, transportation pipelines, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines gather gas from various production sites and transport it to processing plants. Transportation pipelines then transport gas over long distances from processing plants to distribution centers or end-users. Distribution pipelines deliver gas directly to commercial, industrial, and residential customers. Each segment plays a crucial role in the efficient and reliable transportation of natural gas throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
