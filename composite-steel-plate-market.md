<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite steel plate market consists of two main types: explosion bonded and roll bonded. Explosion bonded plates are produced by detonating an explosive charge between the base plate and clad plate, creating a metallurgical bond. Roll bonded plates, on the other hand, are created by rolling the base plate and clad plate together with high pressure and temperature, resulting in a strong bond. Both types of composite steel plates offer unique properties and are used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
