<p><strong>The In Line Ultrasonic Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single/Dual Path</li><li>Multi Path</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single/dual path in-line ultrasonic flow meters use one or two sets of transducers to measure the flow of a liquid. These meters are suitable for applications where space is limited or when the flow rate is relatively low. On the other hand, multi-path in-line ultrasonic flow meters use multiple sets of transducers to provide a more accurate measurement of the flow. They are ideal for applications where high accuracy is required, such as in custody transfer or billing applications.</p></p>
