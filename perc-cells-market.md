<p><p>PERC cells, or Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell technology, are used in solar panels to improve efficiency. In the market, there are two main types: Single Side PERC cells and Double Side PERC cells. Single Side PERC cells have their passivation layer on only one side, improving performance and reducing costs. In contrast, Double Side PERC cells have passivation layers on both the front and back sides, further boosting efficiency and energy output. Both types offer advantages depending on specific needs and budget constraints in the solar industry.</p></p>
