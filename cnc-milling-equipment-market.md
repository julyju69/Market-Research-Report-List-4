<p><p>CNC milling equipment market can be categorized into horizontal and vertical types. Horizontal milling machines have a horizontal spindle, allowing for more complex and heavy-duty operations like cutting grooves and slots. Vertical milling machines have vertical spindles, making them ideal for simpler tasks like drilling or tapping. The horizontal market caters to industries requiring precision and accuracy in large-scale production, while the vertical market serves small to medium-scale businesses focusing on versatility and flexibility in their machining operations.</p></p>
