<p><strong>The Glued Parquet Floor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engineered</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glued parquet floor market consists of two main types: engineered and solid. Engineered glued parquet floors are made from layers of wood stacked on top of each other, providing greater stability and resistance to moisture than solid wood. They are suitable for areas with fluctuating humidity levels. Solid glued parquet floors are made from a single piece of solid wood and offer natural warmth and elegance. They are recommended for areas with stable humidity levels. Both types offer a wide variety of designs, colors, and finishes to suit different preferences and interior styles.</p></p>
