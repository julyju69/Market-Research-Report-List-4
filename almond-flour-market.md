<p><p>Almond flour is available in different types based on the processing method. Blanched almond flour, on the other hand, is made from blanched almonds without skins, resulting in a finer texture and lighter color. Other types of almond flour may include those with added flavors or almond meal which is coarser in texture. Each type of almond flour has its own unique characteristics and uses in cooking and baking.</p></p>
