<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) is commonly available in two main types: Central Tube Structure OPGW and Layer Stranding Structure OPGW. Central Tube Structure OPGW consists of optical fibers enclosed within a central stainless steel tube, providing high tensile strength and protection. On the other hand, Layer Stranding Structure OPGW comprises multiple layers of steel wires wound around the optical fibers, offering better resistance to bending and vibration. Both market types cater to the increasing demand for reliable and efficient communication networks in various industries.</p></p>
