<p><strong>The Plastic Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Coatings</li><li>Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solvent-based coatings in the plastic coatings market use organic solvents to dissolve a resin that forms a protective layer when applied to the surface of a substrate. This type of coating is known for its fast drying time and durability. On the other hand, powder coating involves applying a dry powder to the surface of a substrate, which is then melted and fused into a smooth coating through heat. This method is known for its environmentally friendly properties and ability to produce a thick, uniform coat.</p></p>
