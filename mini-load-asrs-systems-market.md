<p><p>Mini-Load AS/RS Systems are utilized in various industries to automate storage and retrieval processes. Single Shuttle Systems consist of one shuttle that moves horizontally to retrieve and store goods. Dual Shuttle Systems on the other hand have two shuttles that work together to increase efficiency and speed in handling items. Single Shuttle Systems are commonly used in smaller operations while Dual Shuttle Systems are favored in larger warehouses or distribution centers where a higher throughput is needed. Both types offer improved inventory management and space utilization.</p></p>
