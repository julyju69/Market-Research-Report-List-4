<p><p>Asynchronous motors are commonly categorized into two main market types: single-phase and three-phase. Single-phase asynchronous motors are used in applications where only a single-phase power supply is available, such as in household appliances. On the other hand, three-phase asynchronous motors are used in industrial settings where more power is required, such as in pumps, compressors, and conveyor systems. Both types of motors operate using electromagnetic induction to generate rotational motion, making them ideal for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
