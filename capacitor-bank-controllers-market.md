<p><p>Capacitor bank controllers can be categorized into two main types: manual control and automatic control. In manual control, operators manually adjust the settings of the capacitor bank controllers based on system requirements and load conditions. On the other hand, in automatic control, the controller uses sensors and algorithms to automatically regulate the switching of capacitors based on real-time data, optimizing power factor and improving energy efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in managing power quality and reducing electricity costs in industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
