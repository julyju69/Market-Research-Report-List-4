<p><p>The ultrafiltration system market consists of two main types: polymeric and ceramic membranes. Polymeric membranes are made from synthetic materials like polymer and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and flexibility. On the other hand, ceramic membranes are made from inorganic materials like aluminum oxide or zirconium dioxide, offering higher durability and resistance to high temperatures. Both types of membranes are used in various industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals for separating solids from liquids through a filtration process.</p></p>
