<p><p>Hydraulic filters are essential components in hydraulic systems to remove contaminants and ensure proper functioning. The market for hydraulic filters can be classified into different types based on their placement and function. In-line filters are installed within the hydraulic system, while off-line filters are separate units used for additional filtration. Tank filters are placed directly inside the hydraulic tank, spin-on filters are easy to replace, return line filters clean fluid before returning to the tank, and duplex filters provide continuous filtration by using two elements.</p></p>
