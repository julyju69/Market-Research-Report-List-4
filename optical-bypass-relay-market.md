<p><p>Optical Bypass Relay is available in two main types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode relays are designed for long-distance transmission with a single light ray, offering high bandwidth and low signal loss. On the other hand, Multimode relays use multiple light rays for shorter distances, making them suitable for high-speed LAN and data center applications. Both types of relays play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable data transmission in optical networks, catering to different requirements based on the desired transmission distance and speed.</p></p>
