<p><p>Thermal actuators are categorized into two main types based on their default position: Normally Closed Type and Normally Open Type. Normally Closed Type actuators are in a closed position when there is no power supplied, and open when power is applied. On the other hand, Normally Open Type actuators are in an open position when there is no power supplied, and close when power is applied. Both types have their own unique applications and are used in various industries for controlling fluid flow and temperature regulation.</p></p>
