<p><p>Automobile coolant market types can be broadly categorized into four main categories - Inorganic Additive, Organic Acid, Hybrid Organic Acid, and Others. Inorganic Additive coolants typically use ethylene glycol as a base with inorganic additives to prevent corrosion. Organic Acid coolants are based on propylene glycol and organic acids to provide corrosion protection. Hybrid Organic Acid coolants combine both inorganic and organic compounds for enhanced protection. Other types may include specialty coolants for specific applications or using different base ingredients for unique properties.</p></p>
