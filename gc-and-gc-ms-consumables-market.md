<p><p>GC and GC-MS consumables market types include Ion Source, Chromatographic Column, Filter, Injector, and Others. The Ion Source plays a crucial role in ionizing the sample, while the Chromatographic Column separates the components of the sample. Filters are used to remove impurities, and Injectors are responsible for injecting the sample into the system. Other consumables such as tubing, septa, and liners are also essential for maintaining the efficiency and accuracy of the GC and GC-MS systems.</p></p>
