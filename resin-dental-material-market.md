<p><strong>The Resin Dental Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Macrofilled Filler</li><li>Microfilled Filler</li><li>Hybrid Filler</li><li>Nanofilled Filler</li><li>Bulk Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resin dental materials are categorized based on the type of fillers used in the material. Macrofilled filler resins contain larger filler particles for improved strength. Microfilled filler resins have smaller particles for enhanced esthetics. Hybrid filler resins consist of a combination of both large and small particles for a balance of strength and esthetics. Nanofilled filler resins contain nanoscale particles for superior polishability and wear resistance. Bulk filler resins are designed for bulk filling of cavities with minimal shrinkage. Each type offers different advantages for specific dental applications.</p></p>
